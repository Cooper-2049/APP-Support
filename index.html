Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

At ASO Glance, your privacy is our priority. As a tool designed for developers, we respect your data and intellectual property.

1. Data Collection: ASO Glance is a 100% local utility app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your metadata, keyword ideas, or uploaded app screenshots to external servers.

2. Photo Access: Our app requires access to your Photo Library only to allow you to import screenshots for ASO preview purposes. These images are processed entirely on-device.

3. Third-Party Services: This app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that track your behavior.

4. Data Security: All app data is stored securely using the iOS file system (Codable + FileManager). Deleting the app will remove all local data unless backed up via system-wide iCloud backups.
