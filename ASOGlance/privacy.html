ASO Glance Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

ASO Glance is a local productivity tool for organizing App Store keyword ideas, metadata notes, and app listing drafts.

Data Collection

ASO Glance does not require account registration and does not collect personal data from users.

Local Storage

Projects, notes, and app-related information created in ASO Glance are stored locally on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

The current version of ASO Glance does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or social features.

Data Sharing

ASO Glance does not sell user data and does not share personal data with advertisers.
